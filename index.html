Passive Income

Our team knows what it's like for early adopters when it comes to start-up projects. We've already been in your place. That's why we decided to reward our early investors with the opportunity to earn passive income.

That is how it will work:

Those responsible for the first 50 mints of each generation will be promoted to shareholders.

Investors who support our team in the 1st Generation will receive, as a reward, 10% of all DNA Creations revenue

Investors who missed the first opportunity and decided to join us in the 2st Generation will receive, as a reward, 5% of all DNA Creations revenue

And finally, those who saw the success of DNA Creations happening and decided not to miss a last chance will receive, as a reward, 2% of all DNA Creations revenue

When the mint period is over, the addresses of our shareholders' wallets will be stored in the collection contract.
