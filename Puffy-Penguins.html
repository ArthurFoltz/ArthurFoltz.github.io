The puffy penguin's tale

In excavations led by Tatsuo, he had found ancient Egyptian scrolls where the Egyptians told of an advanced society "on the frozen continent that was in the far south, at the end of the world", a few years later after exhaustively searching for some other account of such a civilization elsewhere in the world, he found in Central America an ancient sculpture of Mayan society, which told of a civilization "ahead of ours", and that it was "in the south of the continent after the sea".

T he Japanese archaeologist named Tatsuo Kurama was known by colleagues and universities in the field for his research and wild theories about lost civilizations, he insisted on teaching his students these theories, which was almost causing his dismissal, but Tatsuo was a persistent man and did not give up easily.

There was only one thing that intrigued Tatsuo in these ancient writings, they referred to the citizens of this supposed ancient civilization as "small and hairy", but Tatsuo thought that these were special clothes due to the cold in Antarctica and did not give them much importance. After coming across this latest Mayan sculpture, he wasted no time in organizing an expedition to the place where, according to his lifetime of studies, this civilization was located, and since he was a well-known professor at the Japanese academy, despite his unusual theories, he got the necessary support for his expedition.

These theories began at the age of 16, when he came across an old book in a library that told the story of the lost continent of Atlantis, and from then on he dedicated his life to unraveling the mysteries of these civilizations. But it was not Atlantis that fascinated him, but a supposed lost city in the middle of Antarctica.

After almost an hour of walking, the team began to see stronger and stronger lights and sounds, and what they found when they arrived at the source of the lights made everyone speechless and perplexed. They had found the civilization that Tatsuo had spent his life searching for, and not just any civilization, the Penguin civilization.

Six months had passed since the beginning of his expedition, Tatsuo and his team were already running out of supplies, food was a few days away from ending and ignoring the requests and warnings of the rest of his team, Tatsuo was firm in his idea of going all the way, because according to him the team had already entered the frozen continent and were under places unexplored by mankind, they were the first there, and Tatsuo could feel that they were close to their goal.

When the supplies and food ran out completely, the whole team was desperate, but Tatsuo tried to calm them down, because on the horizon they could see, according to him, the place they risked their lives to find, it was a strange mountain, as if it were something artificial, created not by nature, but by someone. Once there, the team spotted an entrance to the mountain, a sort of shelter from the cold. When they entered this shelter they began to hear strange noises, something like a city, thinking they were going crazy, Tatsuo and his team followed the sound and went deeper and deeper into the cave.
